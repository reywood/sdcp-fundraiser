Raising Money For Our School

This year's Gala will be held on Friday May 19, 2023 from 7:00pm to 10:00pm at Breakfast Company in Mission Valley. Tickets are $70 per person and include admission, appetizers, dinner, dessert, 2 drink coupons, music, dancing and lots of fun! Adults 21 and over are welcome to attend. The Gala will feature a silent auction that includes 2 art pieces from each classroom, Teacher Features and donations from the SDCP community. We will also have a wine & spirits pull and a 50/50 raffle. SDCP parents, guardians, grandparents, friends and other adult family members and community members are invited to attend for a night of fun!

Family members that donate $150 or more will receive a special photo book to thank you for your donation.

Net proceeds earned from the 2023 Gala will be used to send a selection of teachers to an annual conference that is organized through the National Association for the Education of Young Children (NAEYC). This is a professional growth opportunity where educators from around the globe connect, collaborate, and learn together. The conference this year will be held in Nashville, TN on November 15-18, 2023.

Our goal is to raise $15,000 so that a teacher from each of our classrooms can attend.

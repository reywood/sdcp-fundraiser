A Worthy Cause

Please join us on May 15th for an evening of dinner, drinks, and dancing to benefit the amazing teachers at the San Diego Cooperative Preschool.

With your help, we will be able to send our teachers to the world's largest early childhood conference created by the National Association for the Education of Young Children. Here they will be able to connect, collaborate and learn with fellow early childhood educators from around the globe. This annual conference takes place over several days and allows early childhood educators to get up to date with the latest practices in the field, choose from hundreds of innovative sessions to attend, network with other professionals, and reignite their passion for teaching young children.
